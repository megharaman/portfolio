Music

Indian Classical Singing I have been learning Indian Classical Music for 9 years. Through traditional Indian music I am able to take part in my heritage and culture. I have been learning piano since 2008. In 2012, I began working with my singing teacher to combine Western and Traditional Indian Music. I perform recitals multiple times a year. I have been playing clarinet since 2011. I have developed a strong love for the instrument. I am a part of my school's band and marching band programs. Outside of school, I perform classical and traditional pieces on the clarinet. I have learned to play the Nadaswaram, an ancient Indian instrument in the past year.
